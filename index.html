WHAT THIS PROVES

Museum collections are organised by institution. Visitors think by artist.

This open catalogue reorganises collection records around two practical questions: where can I see art by this person, and what can I see where I am going?

The underlying file is intentionally human-readable. Each record contains the work, location, coordinates, attribution status and an official source. That makes it possible to review and improve the evidence through GitHub.

Scope: selected publicly accessible sculptures, paintings and frescoes. Drawings, architectural works, copies, lost works and most disputed attributions are excluded.
